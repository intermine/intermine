Description: Databases cross-referenced in UniProtKB
Name:        dbxref.txt
Release:     2012_11 of 28-Nov-2012

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document lists the databases that are cross-referenced in UniProtKB.
It does not list WWW resources or databases specifically mentioned in the
"WEB RESOURCE" comment topic of UniProtKB entries.

It lists both the databases that are explicitly linked (in the DR lines and
in rare cases in the FT lines) and those to which implicit links can be
automatically generated by programs based on simple rules.

The Db_URL field allows to access an entry in the cross-referenced database
by substituting %s by the first ("primary") identifier found in a
cross-reference line, or %u by the UniProtKB accession number of the entry.

For more details on UniProtKB cross-references, implicit and explicit
links, you can read:

PubMed=11488411;
Gasteiger E., Jung E., Bairoch A.
SWISS-PROT: connecting biological knowledge via a protein database.
Curr. Issues Mol. Biol. 3:47-55(2001)
