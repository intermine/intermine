The results table shows the starting gene, orthologous genes and organism names with details of each GO term applied to the orthologous genes. Orthologue data in FlyMine comes from InParanoid, note that 'main' means the best hit in the other organism, 'secondary' denotes paralogues of the best hit.

Summary buttons at the top of each column allow you to view some summary statistics. For names and identifiers it will tell you about unique values, numerical data gives the min, max, mean and standard deviation.
